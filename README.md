### About me

I'm a self-taught engineer with a BS in Economics & Finance. I build AI systems for businesses — voice agents, chatbots, automation pipelines, and full-stack products — all as a solo operator augmented by AI.

My background in economics means I don't just write code. I understand unit economics, margins, and what it actually takes for a system to generate ROI. Every project I take on has one requirement: **it has to make you money.**
